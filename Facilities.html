Laboratories

Theoretical knowledge is put to test only when it is applied to practical situations. Apeejay School,Salt Lake has state of the art - well equipped laboratories for subjects that need hands-on learning. Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Bio-technology, Geography, Web Multimedia, Computer Science, Atal Tinkering Lab(ATL) & Mathematics are to name a few that are equipped with arrangements to ensure the best teaching learning experience. The Art Room helps nurture the latent skills of budding artists. This room is always abuzz with creative and innovative activities based on Art & Craft.

Infirmary

The school infirmary is managed by a trained nurse to deal with minor emergencies. It has regular and ample supply of first aid and essential medicines. Children falling sick are eased of their discomfort to the best of the ability of the staff nurse. Major ailments / injuries are referred to the Hospital/ Nursing Homes in the vicinity of the school with prior information to the parents of the concerned student.

Canteen

The school canteen is equipped to provide wholesome and healthy yet popular food options to children. Health drinks & soups are common items on the list as are bhelpuri, sandwiches, pastries and etc.
